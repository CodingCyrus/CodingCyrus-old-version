About Me

Introduction

Hello World! My name is Cyrus Chun, I am currently a student in the Masters of Science in Software Development program at Boston University based out of Los Angeles, California. Prior to moving to Los Angeles, I worked as a Brand Marketing Executive at a Global FMCG company after graduating from Waseda University in Japan with a BA in Political Economics of International Development. After taking several coding courses online in Python and Java, I realized I had a passion to learn new technologies but also create innovative solutions through software development. During my time at BU, my goal is to become a Android Mobile Developer in addition to developing the latest skills in Full Stack Java Development through pursuing internships and building personal projects.

Who is Cyrus?

Cyrus was born in Bangkok, Thailand in 1996 to Japanese and Korean parents. He has lived in Bangkok, Thailand for 4 years after which he lived in Los Angeles, USA for 11 years and Tokyo, Japan for 10 years. A born digital nomad, Cyrus is also a native bilingual speaker in English and Japanese but hopes to one day be a native multi-lingual in advanced programming languages. Prior to Covid-19, Cyrus was a avid traveller having visited over +20 countries. But after enrolling in Boston University in Fall 2021, he likes to spend most of his time indoors exploring the world of the internet.
